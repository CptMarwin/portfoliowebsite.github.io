Power Bi Project

This SQL script analyzes HR data to provide insights into the employee demographics, tenure, turnover rates, and geographical distribution. It starts by renaming columns for clarity and cleaning date fields. The script calculates employee age groups, gender distribution, race/ethnicity breakdown, and the number of employees at different locations. It further explores the average length of employment for terminated employees and the distribution of job titles across the company. The turnover rate is assessed across departments, and the geographical distribution is analyzed by state and city. Additionally, the script tracks changes in employee count over time based on hire and termination dates, and it computes the average tenure for each department. Overall, the script delivers a comprehensive overview of HR metrics for strategic decision-making.
